About Risen Christ Fellowship

Since its founding on 20th September 2020, Risen Christ Fellowship has been steadily growing — numerically, spiritually, structurally, and more. We shall continue to grow until we become what God wants us to become.

Our joy is to be compassionate, inclusive, and to walk with Jesus. Through gospel, service, worship, and missions, we bring God closer to our hearts and to those around us.

As a fellowship of believers, we have the honor of proclaiming the glory of Christ to one another in a loving and caring setting.

We are a place of prayer, deliverance, peace, and joy to all who join us. Our community is richly diverse, with people of different ages and backgrounds coming together to worship and serve. We welcome all individuals seeking God’s love — our doors are open to every soul welcoming Jesus into their heart.

At the Heart of Everything We Do

As devoted members of Risen Christ Fellowship, we are constantly pursuing the calling to be fully devoted followers of Christ. To build a true heaven on Earth, we live by the teachings of the Bible, are passionate about prayer, and are grounded in spiritual accountability.

If you are interested in joining our Church, please reach out — we would love for you to visit!
